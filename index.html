Educational reference

This site is for study and planning support. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace care from qualified health professionals.

Modify first

Practices should be adapted to the person, context, current symptoms, medical restrictions, and available support.

Stop signals

Pain, dizziness, numbness, breath strain, pressure, or distress are reasons to pause and reassess.
